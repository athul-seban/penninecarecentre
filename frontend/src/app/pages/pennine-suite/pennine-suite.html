An Introduction to Tasteful Modernity

Nestling in the beautiful market town of Glossop, on the edge of the Peak District National Park, Pennine Care is a newly built, purpose-designed residential care home. Our Pennine Suite offers a warm, welcoming environment where residents feel truly at home.

Thoughtfully designed to blend contemporary comfort with a homely atmosphere, every detail of the Pennine Suite has been crafted with our residents' wellbeing and dignity in mind. From the light-filled communal spaces to the carefully appointed private bedrooms, quality is present at every turn.

We believe that moving into residential care should feel like a positive new chapter — one filled with warmth, connection, and the very best of care. Our dedicated team is on hand around the clock to ensure every resident feels supported, valued, and at ease.

Our Warm & Caring Community

At the heart of the Pennine Suite is a vibrant, caring community where residents are encouraged to live life to the full. Our spacious lounge and dining areas are designed to foster friendship, laughter, and a true sense of belonging.

From shared mealtimes prepared with fresh, seasonal ingredients to a rich programme of activities tailored to individual interests, we ensure every day offers something meaningful. Whether it is a quiet morning with a newspaper or an afternoon of music and reminiscence, life here is enriching.

Our compassionate care team takes the time to truly know each resident — their history, preferences, and what brings them joy — so that care is always personal, respectful, and dignified.

"A place where every resident is known by name, valued as an individual, and treated with the utmost kindness and respect."

Private, Well-Appointed Bedrooms

Each bedroom within the Pennine Suite is a private sanctuary — a space that truly belongs to the resident. Generously proportioned and filled with natural light, our rooms offer a calm and comfortable retreat at any hour of the day.

Residents are warmly encouraged to personalise their rooms with cherished possessions, family photographs, and favourite furnishings, creating a space that reflects their personality and feels unmistakably like home.

Every room features an en-suite wet room, nurse call system, flat-screen television, and individually controlled heating, ensuring comfort, privacy, and independence at every moment.

Tranquil Gardens & Outdoors

Our beautifully maintained gardens offer residents the opportunity to enjoy the fresh Peak District air and the restorative power of nature. With accessible pathways, sheltered seating areas, and seasonal planting, the outdoors feels welcoming all year round.

The gardens have been thoughtfully designed for residents of all mobility levels, with level paving, raised planters, and carefully considered landscaping that invites exploration and quiet contemplation in equal measure.

With sweeping views across the Pennine hills, time spent outdoors at Pennine Care is genuinely uplifting — a natural extension of the warmth and care found within.
