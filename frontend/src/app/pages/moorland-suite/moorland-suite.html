An Elegant Haven for Gentlemen

The Moorland Suite at Pennine Care has been thoughtfully designed as a dedicated space for gentlemen, offering a refined and supportive environment tailored to their individual needs, interests, and sense of independence.

Set within our beautifully designed home on the edge of the Peak District, the Moorland Suite combines the very best of modern residential care with a warmth and homeliness that makes every resident feel truly welcome from the moment they arrive.

Our experienced and compassionate team are on hand around the clock, ensuring every resident receives the personalised care and attention they deserve — always delivered with dignity, respect, and a genuine smile.

Community Spaces

The Moorland Suite's communal areas have been designed to encourage connection, conversation, and a real sense of camaraderie. Our generous lounge and dining spaces provide the perfect backdrop for shared meals, lively discussions, and quiet companionship alike.

From morning newspapers over fresh coffee to afternoon activities, films, and special events, the daily rhythm of life in the Moorland Suite is purposeful, engaging, and — above all — enjoyable. Residents are always at the centre of everything we do.

Our activities programme is tailored to the interests and abilities of residents, ensuring meaningful engagement every day, whether that means a game of cards, a talk on local history, or simply enjoying good company.

Private, Tailored Bedrooms

Each bedroom in the Moorland Suite is a private, well-appointed space where residents can relax, recharge, and feel completely at ease. Spacious and filled with natural light, our rooms are designed to feel like a genuine home from home.

Residents are encouraged to bring their own belongings, photographs, and personal touches to make their room uniquely theirs. We believe strongly that a familiar, personal environment plays a vital role in wellbeing and happiness.

Every room includes a modern en-suite wet room, flat-screen television, nurse call system, and individually controlled heating — all the comforts needed for a relaxed, independent daily life.

Secure, Healing Gardens

Our fully enclosed, secure garden spaces offer residents the freedom to enjoy the outdoors with complete peace of mind. Thoughtfully landscaped with accessible pathways, raised planters, and comfortable seating areas, the gardens are a true extension of the care home experience.

Spending time outdoors has well-documented benefits for physical and emotional health, and we actively encourage residents to make use of these beautiful spaces throughout the seasons. The views across the surrounding moorland are simply breathtaking.

For residents living with dementia, the secure outdoor environment provides a safe and stimulating space for gentle exercise, sensory engagement, and the simple pleasure of fresh air.
