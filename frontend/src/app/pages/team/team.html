Our Team & Values

Who We Are

At Pennine Care Centre, our staff are our greatest asset. We are dedicated to creating a rewarding workplace that translates into world-class care for our residents.

Our Vision

We aspire to consistently develop and deliver compassionate person-centred care services, enabling people to live fulfilled lives and create a rewarding workplace for our staff.

Our Mission

To provide caring services with a focus on compassion, empathy, independence, and choice, meeting the individual needs of our residents within a warm, comfortable, homely environment.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our everyday interactions and long-term goals.

Caring

The wellbeing of our residents is at the heart of everything we do, treating people with dignity and respect, embracing a caring, safe and supportive culture across our staff teams.

Continuity

We strive to provide a quality service through continuous improvement. We reflect & focus our thoughts, energy, and attention on achieving goals whilst embracing innovation to ensure we continually enhance our service.

Collaboration

We commit to working in partnership with residents, loved ones, and professionals. By involving everyone in planning and decision-making, we ensure transparency and continuous standards.

Commitment

We are committed to meeting the bespoke needs of our residents and will behave professionally & responsibly in the delivery of our services, always supporting each other.
